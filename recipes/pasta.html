Penne alla vodka

Easy, delicious penne pasta with homemade sauce.

Ingredients:

  • One pound penne pasta
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 whole medium onion, chopped finely
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup vodka
  • One 14 ounce can tomato paste
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Grated parmesean for serving

Directions:

  1. Begin by boiling and salting water according to directions on your pasta box.
  2. Melt butter over low heat and then add onions and garlic.
  3. Add in tomato paste and stir until the color has darkended. Then add in your vodka.
  4. Reduce heat to low and pour in your heavy cream. While stiring add in your red pepper, cracked pepper and salt to taste.
  5. Finally drain your pasta reserving about 1/4 cup of your pasta water for the sauce.
  6. Mix in cooked pasta and pasta water to your sauce and stir until combined.
  7. Add greated parmesean and serve.
